# V1 grantees are email, npub, and Finite VIP NIP-05

In v1, products may create Product Grants for email addresses and native `npub` identifiers. A NIP-05-shaped grantee is trusted as a NIP-05 Name only when it is on the Finite VIP Domain; other email-shaped identifiers remain Invited Emails, and Third-Party NIP-05 Name resolution is future work.
